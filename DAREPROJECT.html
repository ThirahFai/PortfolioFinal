DARE

DARE is an adventure-educational game that my team and I created based on the theme of educational games. We spent a week developing the game because we needed to make some last-minute changes. I programmed and designed the game, as well as provided various assets. During an event called GameInc, we were nominated for several awards, including the Best Trailer Award, Best Poster Award, and Best Booth Award.
